Our Mission

At Horse Rescue, we strive to give neglected, abandoned, and mistreated horses a second chance. We provide medical care, food, and a safe environment for these beautiful animals to recover. We also work to find loving homes for our horses.

How You Can Help

You can help by adopting, volunteering, or donating to support our cause. Every contribution goes directly to the care of the horses. Visit our 'View Horses' section to meet some of the horses currently up for adoption, or contact us for more ways to get involved.
